Origin Story

In 2035 Entrepreneur and space exploration lover Noah F. Andrson opened H-Thai-ML to serve all the brave astronauts exploring our solar system. Our Original location is 100 miles south of the Apollo 11 landing site. You can also find us near the largest Volcano in the solar system, Olymus Mons, or on the only moon with a thick atmosphere, Titan. If none of those suits your fancy then why not check out our newest location on Pluto.
